How This Works

  1. You tell me about your tech issue.
  2. We pick a good cause you'd like to donate to. I hope you pick one of my favorite causes!
  3. We agree on a rate based on what you are able to contribute. My default rate is $50 per hour.

Donate To Some Good Causes

I selected a list of good causes that address a variety of issues I think are very important: Climate Change, Money in Politics, Health Care, Housing, and Independent Journalism.

Rainforest Foundation US

The Rainforest Foundation US is a non-profit organization dedicated to protecting rainforests in Central and South America. They achieve this by supporting Indigenous communities who are actively conserving these vital ecosystems. Through legal advocacy, land protection, and empowerment initiatives, they contribute to rainforest preservation and the well-being of Indigenous peoples in the region.

Democracy Now!

Democracy Now! is a daily, global, independent news program hosted by award-winning journalists Amy Goodman and Juan González. Their reporting covers breaking daily news headlines and in-depth interviews with people on the front lines of the world’s most pressing issues.

National Alliance to End Homelessness

The National Alliance to End Homelessness is a nonpartisan organization committed to preventing and ending homelessness in the United States. As a nonprofit, they provide resources, training, advocacy, and data on homelessness issues, policies, and solutions. Their work informs policymakers and elected officials, aiming to create a society where everyone has a safe and stable place to call home.

Brennan Center for Justice

The Brennan Center for Justice is a nonpartisan organization dedicated to protecting democracy, justice, and liberty in the United States. They focus on issues like voting rights, criminal justice reform, and campaign finance. Their mission is to create a more democratic, just, and free America for all.

Physicians for a National Health Program

Physicians for a National Health Program (PNHP) is an advocacy organization comprising over 20,000 American physicians, medical students, and health professionals. Their mission centers around supporting a universal, comprehensive single-payer national health insurance program. In essence, they advocate for a system where everyone has access to medical care, regardless of their financial status or background. Through research, education, and policy advocacy, PNHP strives to transform the U.S. health care system into one that prioritizes equitable and accessible health care for all.
